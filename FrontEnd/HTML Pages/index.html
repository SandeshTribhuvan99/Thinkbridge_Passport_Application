Welcome to the Passport Application Portal

The Passport Application Portal by the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, is your one-stop solution for managing all passport-related services. Below, you will find detailed information about the various services and features available on this portal.

Application

The Application section allows you to apply for a new passport or renew your existing one. Choose from the following options:

  • New Passport: Fill out the application form to apply for a new passport.
  • Renew Passport: Renew your existing passport by submitting the necessary details.

Application Status

Track the status of your passport application through the Application Status section. The options available are:

  • All Previous Applications: View the history of all your previous passport applications.
  • Current Application Status: Check the status of your current passport application in real-time.

Application Payment

Manage all your payment-related tasks for passport applications in the Application Payment section. The options include:

  • Calculate Fee: Use our calculator to estimate the fee for your passport application.
  • Make Payment: Proceed to make the payment for your passport application fee online.
  • Check Payment Status: Verify the status of your payment to ensure it has been processed successfully.

Update Application Details

If you need to make changes to your application details, visit the Update Application Details section. The options provided are:

  • Make Changes: Edit the details of your current application if needed.
  • Check Status: Check the status of the approval process for the changes you have made.

Feedback and Queries

We value your feedback and are here to assist you with any queries. The Feedback and Queries section includes:

  • Submit Feedback: Provide your feedback to help us improve our services.
  • FAQs: Browse through frequently asked questions to find quick answers.
  • Need Help? Contact our support team for any assistance you require.
